Our Process

Consultation & Strategy

I'll talk to you by phone or in person to get an in-depth understanding of your business. I want to know about your customers, your strengths, challenges, sales-process and more. This helps me determine the best marketing strategy for the design of your website.

Build & Refine

From there, I will build your site based on the information we discussed in the initial consultation. From there we will get back together to fix any errors or adjust anything that needs to be changed. I want to make sure you're happy with the finished product.

Launch & Support

When we are ready we will launch your site. You will maintain complete ownership and control of the site. I won't leave you hanging if you have questions and, if necessary, I will make any updates beyond the initial build for a reasonable hourly fee.

About Us

What Drives Our Work

  • Ethos

    Every business is different. Different target customer, different strengths, different challenges, different goals. So websites can't be just a one-size-fits-all proposition. We design with your specific goals and outcomes in mind.

  • Method

    Maybe back in 1999 it was enough just to have a website. Not anymore. No matter what your business is, your customers are looking for you online. After our initial consultation with you, we design your website to attract the kind of business you want and provide a coherent look and feel that communicates who you are as a business.

  • Approach

    Many designers are concerned only with how a website looks. This is important to us as well, but we also focus heavily on what your website says to the customer when they visit. Do the images convey the message you want to get across? Do the words on the site tell them what they need to know and how to take the next step? We make sure this happens.

  • Freedom

    To us, freedom is the ability to run your business the way you want, to be successful and ultimately, to intentionally live the life you want. Our businesses exist to serve our lives, not the other way around. At Freedom Web Design & Marketing, we want to help make this your reality
